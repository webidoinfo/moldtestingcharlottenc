Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a mold inspection?

A professional mold inspection includes visual inspection, moisture readings, air quality testing, and surface sampling when necessary. Our inspectors use thermal imaging to detect hidden moisture issues and provide a comprehensive report of findings.

How long does mold testing take?

A typical mold inspection takes 1-2 hours, depending on the size of your property. The laboratory analysis of samples takes 3-5 business days, after which you'll receive a detailed report.

Do you provide mold remediation services?

Yes, we offer professional mold remediation services using eco-friendly solutions. Our team is certified and experienced in safely removing mold and preventing future growth.

What areas do you serve in NC?

We provide mold testing and inspection services throughout Charlotte, Concord, Gaston, and surrounding areas in North Carolina.

How do I know if I need mold testing?

If you notice visible mold growth, musty odors, water damage, or experience unexplained health issues, it's recommended to schedule a professional mold inspection. Early detection can prevent more serious problems.
